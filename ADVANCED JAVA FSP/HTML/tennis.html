About Tennis

Tennis is a fast-paced individual or doubles sport played on various surfaces such as grass, clay, or hard courts. The game involves players using a racket to hit a ball over a net, aiming to land it within the opponent’s court boundaries in a way that makes it difficult for the opponent to return. Points are scored when the opponent fails to return the ball, and the player who wins the most sets takes the match. Tennis requires agility, precision, and mental focus, with prestigious tournaments like Wimbledon, the US Open, and the French Open showcasing the sport’s elite talents.
