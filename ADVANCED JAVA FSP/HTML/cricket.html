About Cricket

Cricket is a bat-and-ball game played between two teams, each consisting of eleven players. Originating in England, it has become one of the most popular sports in countries like India, Australia, and South Africa. The game is played on a circular field with a pitch at its center, where the bowler delivers the ball to the batsman. The batsman's goal is to score runs by hitting the ball and running between wickets, while the opposing team tries to limit the runs and dismiss the batsmen through various methods. Cricket has multiple formats, from the five-day Test matches to the fast-paced Twenty20 games, each requiring a unique strategy and skill set.
